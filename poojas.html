Common Offering Items

  • Fresh flowers and garlands
  • Fruits (especially banana, apple, and coconut)
  • Kumkum, turmeric, and sandalwood paste
  • Incense sticks and camphor
  • Betel leaves and nuts
  • Pure ghee for lamps
  • Red cloth for the goddess
  • Sweets like jaggery, sugar candy, and honey

For specific rituals and poojas, additional items may be required. Please consult with the temple priests for detailed information.
